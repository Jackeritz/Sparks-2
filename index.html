About

This bot allows users to earn and manage Credits within your Discord server. It features fun commands, customizable settings, and a robust banking system.

Commands

Basic Commands

  • !play: Earn Credits.
  • !eternal: A chance to earn a small or large amount of Credits.
  • !infernal: A chance to gain or lose a small amount of Credits.
  • !freeplay: A 50/50 chance to win or lose Credits.
  • !balance: Check your balances.

Banking

  • !deposit <amount>: Deposit Credits.
  • !withdraw <amount>: Withdraw Credits.

Admin Commands

  • !setcurrencyname <name>: Set the currency name.
  • !addcommand <command> <response>: Add a custom command.
